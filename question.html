About EVEV


"EVEV" is the platform that allows to post any event details, further users especially for the university students can view and apply to join in even if it is hold in different organisation. The most consequential purpose of our site is definitely providing the opportunity for the participants to make new friends but also letting user to modify the schedule easily. In addition, it could be useful for the organiser to plan the event by understanding how many people comes and their request as well. In other words, our web system supports event activity organisation, such as university community as main target and improve the efficiency to involve with it for the outsider.
